About Me

Hi! My name is Nakul and I'm a Masters Student at the University of Toronto studying Operations Research and Machine Learning. Prior to that, I was an undergraduate student at Purdue University (Boiler Up!) studying Industrial Engineering with a minor in Computer Science. My academic interests include Optimization and its interactions with Machine Learning. I also like working with Natural Language Processing and its applications on structuring un-structured data.

I'm always interested in taking projects or meeting people. I also do pro-bono data analytics for charities and non-profits. Email me at nakulupadhya1@gmail.com to get in contact with me.
